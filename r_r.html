Rules and Regulations for Renters
  • Quiet time is 10:00pm - 8:00am.

  • Speed limit is 10 miles per hour.

  • Only two vehicles may be parked on each site, on cement.
    Extra vehicles must be parked in the visitorâ€™s parking lot.

  • Keep site in an orderly manner.
    No camping tents allowed.

  • Pool hours are 10:00am - 10:00pm.
    Swim at your own risk.
    No pets, glass containers, food or smoking in the fenced in area.

  • No motorcycles, trail bikes, go carts or anything two or three wheeled with a motor allowed in the park except for entering and exiting.

  • Do not walk through any other site.

  • Two pets maximum allowed per site.
    Keep them on a leash (6 ft. max), clean up after them and control excess barking.
    Use pet designated areas for walking. See bulletin board for areas.

  • Trash is picked up daily for overnight renters of vacant lots.
    For long term stays, and for renters of park models, pick up is on Sundays and Thursdays ONLY.
    Put trash in plastic bags and set in front of lot by 11:00am.
    Dumpsters are located on the dirt road southeast of the pond.

  • Do not allow preschool children to go to the restrooms or playground alone.
