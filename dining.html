Dining

Dining in Northern Michigan

Northern Michigan is home to many restaurants. These two resources will help you find the one you are looking for.

The Northern Michigan Dining Guide lists many of the region's restaurants along with their menus.

Or check out MyNorth.com for a comprehensive listing of restaurants in Emmet, Cheboygan and Charlevoix counties. If you plan on venturing further, you can expand the search to include all of Northern Michigan.
